BAWRA Season Championships

BAWRA declares Men, Women and Mixed team Champions for both Spring and Fall seasons. Additionally, in the Fall, an overall Club Champion is declared.

In 2003 BAWRA switched from a "high point" to a "low point" scoring system to determine Spring and Fall Championships:

1. Points assigned equals the place finished for teams that start and finish their event.

2. Teams are given points for last place plus 1 point that:
--Are Disqualified (DQ)
--Did Not Finish (DNF) (started, but did not finish the race)
--Did Not Start (DNS)
